  • “EmbraceEase- Your Personal Oasis in Virtual Landscape”
    2024 International Conference on Advances in Modern Age Technologies for Health and Engineering Science (AMATHE)
    EmbraceEase is an AI-driven mental wellness chatbot that offers personalized support through intelligent question generation based on user categories like age, gender, and profession. It identifies mental health levels and provides tailored remedies accordingly. The chatbot maintains user privacy, enabling those hesitant to speak out to seek help safely. With continuous learning, it adapts and evolves to enhance user experience and effectiveness. EmbraceEase combines AI, Machine Learning, and NLP in a user-friendly platform for responsive and impactful mental health support.
